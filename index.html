Think you might have anxiety?

You are not alone.

It is normal to experience anxiety now and then, but excessive anxiety is destructive and greatly hinders a person's ability to function.

If you answer yes to most of the items on this list, you probably have anxiety and should seek professional help.

  1. Obsessive Thoughts
  2. Irritability
  3. Unable to Focus
  4. Tense Muscles
  5. Insomnia
  6. Fatigue
  7. Avoidance
  8. Fearing the worst
  9. Anxiety Attacks
  10. Irrational Phobias
